I'm currently working on a production monitoring system called xMost, and the newly released warehouse monitoring system xStock.

xMost

xMost is a system monitoring system, that tracks your work machines and workers 24/7. You as a owner or CEO will have a overview of it all from the comfort of your home. The system works by attaching a small computer or terminal to your machines, these computers will track the consumption of electricity, number of made products, OEE and a lot of other things. There can be made specific reports and statistics just for your needs.

xStock

On the other hand xStock is a warehouse monitoring system. It will take care of tracking all of your incoming and outgoing stock. This works by terminals that warehouse worker will take with them on their shift and when new batch of material or stocks comes in they'll just simply scan a barcode, serial number or batch number. All this you can watch from home or your office, maybe on a vacation it doesn't really matter as long as you'r connected to the internet!
